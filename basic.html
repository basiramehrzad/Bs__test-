Development of a state-of-the-art Online Learning Platform

- A UN Project

We pride ourselves in being the most trusted, best-in-class, and developing state-of-the-art Online Learning Platform for the Deputy Ministry of Municipalities (DMM), Independent Directorate of Local Governance (IDLG) in Kabul, Afghanistan. This project provides a fully functional Online Learning Platform to build the administrative and technical capacity of DMM staff and provide access to municipal staff remotely to develop their capacity via online courses. This project was funded by the UNDP.
This is the first adaptive adult learning system in Afghanistan, providing a large number of digital learning content with real-time feedback, and digital curriculum with embedded assessments. Educators use this platform as part of their instruction to make learning more exciting and interactive for students and to offer lessons that can be personalized for each learner.
